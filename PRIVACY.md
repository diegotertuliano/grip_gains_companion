# Privacy Policy

**Grip Gains Unofficial Companion**

Last updated: December 2025

## Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

## Bluetooth

This app uses Bluetooth solely to communicate with your Tindeq Progressor device. No Bluetooth data is stored or transmitted externally.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on the GitHub repository.
